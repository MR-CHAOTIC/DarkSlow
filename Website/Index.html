DarkSlow

About

DarkSlow is a program for organizations to filter web content for free. The reason we
created DarkSlow is that software like ours where expensive, and in our mines, the customer
should get the best benefits possible without having to pay large amounts.

Goals

DarkSlow's goals are to make a cleaner web environment in companies and be lower
priced than our competitors. Will reach these goals by working and posting updates for feedback regularly.
